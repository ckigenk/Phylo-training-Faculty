Phylogenetics Training at Moi University

The goal of this course is to provide an introduction to the use of phylogenetic tools in teaching.

Course Outcomes:

  • Gain an understanding of the fundamental principles of phylogenetics and its various applications.
  • Acquire the skills to effectively use phylogenetic tools in research and education.
  • Develop an understanding of evolutionary models and learn how to interpret phylogenetic trees.

About the Training

Date: Jan 22, Jan 29 & Feb 6, 2025

Location: Moi University, College of Health Sciences, Main Campus, Eldoret, Kenya

Eligibility

The goal of the course is to provide an introduction for how to use phylogenetic tools in teaching

Topics Covered

  • Introduction to Phylogenetics
  • Tools and Software for Tree Construction
  • Practical Exercises on Sequence Alignment and Tree Building
